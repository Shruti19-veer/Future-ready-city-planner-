The Future Ready City Planner is a next-generation urban planning simulation tool designed to empower students, professionals, and visionaries in creating smart, sustainable, and resilient cities.

üìú City Planning Rules & Guidelines

  • Green Space Accessibility: Every residential zone must be within 500 meters of a public park or green area.
  • Minimum Park Coverage: At least 15% of your total city area should be dedicated to parks and natural landscapes.
  • Public Transportation Reach: Ensure 90% of the population has access to public transit within walking distance.
  • Mixed-Use Development: Encourage neighborhoods that combine residential, commercial, and recreational areas for better walkability.
  • Sustainable Energy: Your city must derive at least 40% of its energy from renewable sources like solar, wind, or hydro.
  • Pollution Control: Industrial zones must be placed away from residential areas and close to pollution management facilities.
  • Emergency Infrastructure: Hospitals and emergency services must be evenly distributed and accessible across all major zones.
